SangoNMT: Fine-Tuning NLLB-200 for Sango-French Machine Translation

Building the first dedicated NLP resources for Sango, the national language of the Central African Republic (5.5M speakers). Created a novel 21,125-pair parallel corpus, fine-tuned NLLB-200 with LoRA to achieve +832% BLEU improvement, and built a translation + speech demo.
